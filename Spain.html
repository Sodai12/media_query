Andalusia · Spain
On my last trip to Andalusia, I found myself captivated by Granada, a suburb that gleams with Spain's Moorish history. Walking the cobbled streets of the Albaicín, felt like time-traveling. One evening, sitting atop of the village, I was gifted with a sunset that cast the Alhambra palace in a golden hue. The combination of the Albaicín's white-washed buildings, the silhouette of the palace, and the setting sun was nothing short of enchanting. Granada's beauty is not just in its architecture, but in these quiet memories and its accumulation of history.
